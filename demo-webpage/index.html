Footstep Reinforcement Learning

Presentation video

Abstract

In simulation of virtual crowds, the steering algorithm govern the agents’ navigation. Previous steering algorithms uses particle or force vector to represent the agent, which is very abstract. In contrast, the footstep model represents agents as two feet and internally respects the locomotion constraints of the human agent’s bipedal model. In the paper, heuristic is used for the footstep model to generate footstep in response to the environment and the goal of the agent. However, a comprehensive heuristic is difficult to develop due to the complexity of the environment. Thus, we proposed producing a Reinforcement Learning (RL) model to replace the crafted heuristic algorithm for steering the footstep model.
